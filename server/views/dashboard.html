Twenty-Thirty Admin

Manage the campaign story, product presentation, vote records, and customer follow-up from one editorial control surface. Shopify handles the purchase and receipt. This admin handles the campaign layer that sits after the sale.

Operating Rhythm
1. Orders In
Import Shopify customer orders or work from the exact customer email after checkout.
2. Campaign Layer
Update products 1-6, upload new imagery, and keep the homepage presentation synced with the drop.
3. Vote Activation
Send the post-purchase vote email from this app, then track token usage and vote participation below.
Design language intentionally mirrors the campaign homepage: mono body type, white editorial surfaces, quiet borders, and restrained red accents.
